Gobang Game

Rule: The goal of the game is to align 5 of your chess pieces consecutively in a line, either vertically, horizontally or diagonally on the board. You will start the game by placing your black chess piece wherever you want on the board. Then the computer will place a white chess. Following that, you two will take turns. You may try to block the computer while building your own five-in-a-line. Whoever aligns the 5 first wins the game.
