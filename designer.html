"Besides whether it works, I also care about whether it looks nice." That is what I always think, which may be different from most engineers.

For me, UI designing isn't about making a complicated looking website with a bunch of flashing effects, but creating a user interface that is extremely user-friendly, which indicates, it should be easy to navigate, and makes user feel comfortable looking at it.

For me, making things look nice is what I am passionate about. Tiny things like one or two pixels, color selection, color contrast, border radius on buttons all contribute to the perfect look of a website. And I am the one who is willing to spend a long time making the UI as good as it can be.
